Who Is Eggtronic (and their newly launched Einova brand)?

Eggtronic is on a mission; To power the world. Literally.

We are a global CE innovator delivering cutting-edge products positioned for the future. While we’re known globally for our amazing wireless power products, that’s just the beginning.

The company was founded in 2012 with offices in the US, Italy and China, but our rapid growth creates a vibrant start-up culture with a #getsh*tdone atmosphere.

If you're looking for that fast-paced, high-impact, quick decisions and small team work environment - then we should talk; The opportunities are limitless!

In This Role You Get To:

  • Close key retail accounts and manage sales programs for Eggtronic and their newly launched Einova brand.

  • Manage and grow distribution channels. Understand and be able to implement various retail and distribution pricing models.

  • Create strategies to maximize sell through, profit, revenue and market share for the short term as well as future growth. (All about the growth!)

  • Develop/negotiate terms to effectively manage long-term relationships and profitability.

  • Work across many departments from product to finance, some of which are international... (so get a good Zoom background picked out!)

  • Work collaboratively with vendors and agencies, such as outside rep firms, to ensure that goals are being met and value is added to the growth of the business.

  • Use your awesome experience and brain to analyze industry trends, competition, products, and pricing.

  • Review/analyze daily sales results comparing actual v. projected results (because reality only bites when you ignore it!).

  • Some domestic travel - (with the opportunity for some international travel - when the world gets back to normal!)

Requirements for Crushing It:

  • Minimum five years experience selling CE into retail and eCommerce accounts

  • Four-year degree required

  • An understanding of key channels and retailers in the US & Canada

  • High degree of proficiency in MS Office.

  • High degree of comfort/experience with retailer back-end portals and reporting

  • Strong financial literacy and financial management skills (You own your P&L!)

  • Solid business planning acumen.

  • Consistent and transparent communication at all times.

  • Ability to perform domestic (and potentially some international) travel.

What You’re Getting Into:

Eggtronic is a start-up; so bring your creativity, your sense of humor and juggling skills. We have an unbelievable team assembled in each area of the business and we’re all here to win! So if you're ready for a fun and exciting day-to-day where the whole team is racing forward to solve problems and remove obstacles to growth, then this might be a fit. Maybe we should connect and find out?
